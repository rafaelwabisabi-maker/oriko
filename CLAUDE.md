# ORIKO

> Orgone art + AI intention research. Two interlocking components: BLESS.EXE (networked blessing platform) + ORGONE.AI (plant biology experiment). Submitted to EMAP 2026 (Ars Electronica BioLab).

## What
ORIKO unifies two works:
- **BLESS.EXE** — web platform where users submit intentions; AI generates tradition-appropriate blessings (7 traditions: Catholic, Islamic, Jewish, Buddhist, Umbanda, Spiritist, Indigenous Amazonian); geolocated live feed
- **ORGONE.AI** — 60-day controlled plant study testing whether AI trained on prayer corpora produces biologically measurable EM outputs vs. neutral AI (PEMF literature as scientific base; Reich accumulator as experimental apparatus)

Canonical reference: `ORIKO-PROJECT-DEFINITION.md` (~4,300 words). All grant apps + statements derive from that file.
