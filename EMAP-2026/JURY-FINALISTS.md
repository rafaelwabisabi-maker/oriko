### PROPOSAL TEXT (100-word short description)

PRAYER RESIDUE is a comparative biological experiment testing whether AI systems trained on different corpora — sacred texts from seven prayer traditions versus neutral language — produce electromagnetically distinguishable outputs that living plants can detect. Two AI models, same architecture, different training data. Both generate low-frequency electromagnetic signals. Two identical plant groups grow in the Ars Electronica BioLab for sixty days — one exposed to the prayer-trained signal, one to the neutral signal. Sensors measure growth rate, bioelectric activity, and chlorophyll content daily. The installation displays the live data as two curves that may or may not diverge.
